  • Milestone 5

There’s a lot of things smart TVs has that flat TVs don’t have like an internet browser, built in apps, and streaming services. Smart TVs also allow you to access more channels, more programs, and music without having to subscribe to a cable service. When phones were invented, especially phones like the iPhone, it took everyone by storm and TVs were forgotten about, but smart TVs made people want TVs again. Some smart TVs has the feature to do a voice search when you want to search for a show and pause or play whatever you are watching. Some smart TVs will let you use your phone as an remote for the smart TV. There are many benefits of having a smart TV like having wires all around your TV. Smart TVs also has great graphics and some come with their own sound surround system.
